the loop

Draw a hand. Call your conviction. Clear the blind.

DRAW
Each round is a blind with a score target. Draw eight cards, play poker hands for chips × multiplier, and spend winnings on jokers that bend the rules in your favor.
CONVICTION
Before you bank a hand, bet LOW / MED / HIGH. Read it right and it multiplies; misjudge it and you pay. That self-read is the whole game — and it's fun with no study content at all.
STUDY (OPTIONAL)
Load a pack and posting a hand asks a question. Answer right to bank it — and your conviction becomes how sure you are of the answer. Confidence is scored, and misses come back on a spaced schedule.
